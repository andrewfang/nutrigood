Mission

Our mission is to help our customers maintain a well balanced diet without having to manually log each meal. We provide a service to the elderly that allows them to choose a set meal plan, which is guaranteed to contain all the macro and micro-nutrients that are essential to a well-balanced diet. The ingredients for these meals are delivered, allowing our customers to flex their culinary muscles.
